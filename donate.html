Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! The NicTilliman Foundation Inc is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN: 39-3751590). All donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for tax purposes.

How is my donation used?

Your donation goes directly to supporting our community programs in High Springs. We prioritize transparency and ensure that funds are used efficiently to maximize impact. Specific allocations include family support services, youth programs, community events, and operational costs necessary to deliver our mission.

Can I donate in honor or memory of someone?

Absolutely. We welcome tribute donations and can provide acknowledgment to the family or individual you designate. Please include a note with your donation or contact us to arrange a memorial or honorary gift.

Do you accept recurring donations?

Yes, recurring donations help us plan and sustain our programs year-round. Please contact us to set up a monthly or quarterly giving plan that works for you.

Can I volunteer instead of donating money?

Absolutely! Volunteers are essential to our work. We have opportunities for individuals, families, and groups. Visit our Events page or contact us to learn about current volunteer needs.

How can my business partner with the foundation?

We welcome corporate partnerships! Options include event sponsorships, employee volunteer programs, matching gift programs, and in-kind donations. Contact us to discuss partnership opportunities.

Ready to Make an Impact?

Every contribution brings us closer to a stronger, more compassionate High Springs. Thank you for considering a donation to The NicTilliman Foundation Inc.
