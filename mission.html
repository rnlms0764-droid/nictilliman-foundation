Our Purpose

Mission Statement

The NicTilliman Foundation Inc brings mental health awareness to first responders and veterans, honoring the legacy of Deputy Sheriff Nic Tilliman through service, education, and community support.

This mission guides every decision we make and every program we develop. It reflects our commitment to being a catalyst for positive change in our community.

Looking Forward

Our Vision

We envision a High Springs where every resident has access to the resources, support, and opportunities they need to thrive.

Our vision is a community where:

  • First responders have access to mental health resources
  • Veterans receive the support they need
  • Mental health awareness is prioritized for those who serve
  • No hero faces their struggles alone
  • The community comes together to support its protectors

How We Help

Our Focus Areas

We concentrate our efforts on key areas where we can make the greatest impact

01

Mental Health Awareness

Bringing critical mental health awareness and resources to first responders and veterans who serve our community.

  • Mental health education programs
  • Resource connections
  • Community outreach
02

First Responder Support

Supporting police, fire, EMS, and other first responders with mental health resources and community recognition.

  • Mental health resource referrals
  • Community appreciation events
  • Peer support connections
03

Veteran Support

Honoring and supporting veterans with mental health awareness and community connection opportunities.

  • Veteran mental health resources
  • Community events
  • Peer support networks
04

Community Events

Hosting events that bring the community together while supporting our mission, like Hot Rodding For Heroes Car Show.

  • Hot Rodding For Heroes Car Show
  • Community fundraisers
  • Awareness campaigns
