Volunteer With Us

Our events and programs wouldn't be possible without dedicated volunteers. Whether you have an hour or a day, your time makes a real difference.

Volunteering with The NicTilliman Foundation Inc is a rewarding way to give back to your community. We have opportunities for individuals, families, and groups.

Ways to Volunteer:

  • Event setup and coordination
  • Community outreach
  • Administrative support
  • Program assistance
  • Special skills (photography, design, etc.)
