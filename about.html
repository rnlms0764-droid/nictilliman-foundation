Our Story

A Legacy of Service

The NicTilliman Foundation Inc was founded in loving memory of Deputy Sheriff Nic Tilliman (February 12, 1985 – February 11, 2023).

Established to honor Nic's commitment to serving others, our foundation brings mental health awareness to first responders and veterans in the High Springs community and beyond. We believe those who protect and serve our communities deserve support for their own wellbeing.

Through community events, outreach programs, and partnerships with local organizations, we work to ensure that no first responder or veteran faces mental health challenges alone.

Our Inspiration

In Loving Memory

Deputy Sheriff Nic Tilliman (February 12, 1985 – February 11, 2023)

Nic dedicated his life to serving and protecting his community. His commitment to helping others continues to inspire our foundation's work in mental health awareness for first responders and veterans.

The NicTilliman Foundation Inc carries forward his legacy by ensuring those who serve our communities receive the mental health support and awareness they deserve.
